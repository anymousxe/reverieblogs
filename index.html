First Entry — Let’s See Where This Goes

March 12, 2016

I’ve never been the blogging type, but here we are. I’ll probably write about games, small town life, and random thoughts. Nothing special. Just somewhere to keep track of my days.

A Channel I’ve Been Watching Lately

August 3, 2016

There’s this YouTube creator named Aaron Denvil who makes long breakdowns of weird horror games. Dude’s criminally underrated. Might write about some of his videos here from time to time.

Aaron Posted Something Strange

October 17, 2017

Aaron’s recent video felt… off. He kept mentioning some “middlemen.” Not sure if it was lore or a bit. Might be nothing, but it’s been stuck in my head.

He’s Gone.

July 15, 2018

Everyone online is talking about it. Aaron Denvil vanished. I don’t like that word. “Vanished.” People don’t just vanish.

Trying to Make Sense of It

December 4, 2018

I’ve been reading forums, archived tweets, anything. Nothing leads anywhere. Whatever happened, it wasn’t random.

I Think Someone’s Watching the Forums

May 22, 2019

Threads about Aaron keep getting wiped. Not locked — erased. I posted a theory and the whole thread disappeared in under a minute. A few other people messaged me saying it happened to them too.

It Feels Bigger Than I Thought

August 11, 2020

Some accounts that used to talk about Aaron have stopped posting entirely. One of them DM’d me before they went silent. They said: “Stop looking. You don’t know who you’re dealing with.”

Something Isn’t Right

February 9, 2021

I keep seeing the same symbol on random sites. A black triangle, an eye, and three letters: TCI. No idea what it means yet.

This Rabbit Hole Is Getting Dangerous

October 3, 2022

I’m starting to get emails from blank senders. Just timestamps. No subject. No message. I’ve blocked every address but they keep coming.

I Have a Lead on Aaron.

January 27, 2023

I don’t want to say too much here. But I found something. Something big. If this is real, everything people assumed about his disappearance is wrong. I’ll follow up soon.
