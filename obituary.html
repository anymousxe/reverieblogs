In Loving Memory

Daniel Reed Reverie · 1999–2024
Daniel R. Reverie, 25, passed away unexpectedly on March 2, 2024 in what authorities described as a “single-vehicle accident” outside of Red Ridge. Born on April 18, 1999, Daniel was known for his curiosity, his quiet humor, and his dedication to documenting strange corners of internet culture.
He maintained a small personal journal blog from 2016 to early 2023, where he often wrote about online communities, missing-media rabbit holes, and the work of independent creators. His writing touched many people who shared his fascination with the forgotten and unexplained.
Daniel is survived by close friends and extended family who remember him as thoughtful, persistent, and endlessly inquisitive. A private memorial service will be held at a later date.
“Those who wander into the unknown leave more footprints than they ever realize.”
